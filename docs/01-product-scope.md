COLAS QA Product Scope
Platform
COLAS QA is a field-quality platform. The first production module is Lot Pack.
Primary users
Field Worker
Supervisor
QA Reviewer
Administrator
First release capabilities
Secure authentication with persistent device sessions
Create, edit, save, submit, and recover Lot Packs
Extended offline operation
Durable on-device submission queue
Automatic and manual retry after connectivity returns
Server-confirmed upload with duplicate prevention
Attachments, signatures, diagrams, and calculations
Controlled PDF generation
Automatic delivery to configured work email recipients
Manager review and audit trail
Explicitly out of scope for the first release
General company document management
Payroll or HR functions
Replacing corporate identity providers before SSO is approved
Broad analytics unrelated to Lot Pack completion and compliance
Success definition
A worker can complete and submit a Lot Pack with no reception, retain it safely for an extended offline period, and have the exact same submission reach the cloud and designated office recipients once reception returns—without creating a duplicate and without relying on the worker to manually reconstruct or email the pack.
